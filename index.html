MDTraj¶

MDTraj is a python library for loading, saving, and manipulating molecular dynamics trajectory files.

Features¶

MDTraj currently supports loading and saving trajectories in the following formats.

  • PDB
  • GROMACS XTC and TRR
  • CHARMM / NAMD DCD
  • AMBER binpos, NetCDF
  • MDTraj HDF5

The MDTraj geometry library gives you access to fast cartesian root-mean square deviation (RMSD) distances, the calculation of bond lengths, bond angles, dihedral angles, non-redundant internal coordinates, and radius of gyration.
